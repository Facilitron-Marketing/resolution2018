Our story

20+ Years of excellence

Taylor opened doors under the name Resolution Digital Type and Image in 1993 with an AGFA imagesetter and a dream. He quickly became the preferred prepress and design partner of many local and regional businesses.

Slowly but surely the word got out that Resolution provided great graphic design in addition to prepress and Taylor built a loyal base of happy clients. He ran Resolution as a very successful one-man shop specializing in print design and production without so much as a sign on the door.

Twenty years later, there was a homecoming of sorts when Ian returned to Resolution as a partner and brought all he had learned about advertising, marketing, web design, and development.

Together, they re-envisioned Resolution and set out on a mission to provide the best websites and graphic design possible to results-driven clients. Today they operate as a full-service design agency intent on getting real results for their clients.

Taylor Little

Founder and Partner

Taylor has done design work for R.E.M., Widespread Panic and Kenny Rogers; served as consultant for the Lamar Dodd School of Art, House of Blues Entertainment, and Printroom.com; participated on Grammy-nominated projects including R.E.M. albums Monster and New Adventures in Hi-Fi; and worked on motion picture projects including Man in the Moon and The Virgin Suicides.
